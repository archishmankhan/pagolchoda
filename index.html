Customer Base
All about our customer

The customer base for a paying guest business model primarily comprises students, working professionals, and migrants seeking affordable and convenient short to medium-term accommodation. Students often opt for PG accommodations near educational institutions for a comfortable and secure living environment. Working professionals choose PGs for their proximity to workplaces and cost-effectiveness. Migrants, on the other hand, rely on PGs as a transitional housing solution while they establish themselves in a new city. Understanding and catering to the unique needs of these diverse customer segments is crucial for the success of a PG business.
